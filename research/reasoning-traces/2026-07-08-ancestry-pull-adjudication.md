# Ancestry subscription pull: adjudication (2026-07-08)

Owner-authorized single-session pull of their paid Ancestry account, 15 sheet
targets + the raw captures in research/pulls/ancestry-2026-07/ (gitignored;
Ancestry ToS). This trace adjudicates the haul against the page. Two crown
findings were re-read from the saved scans by the driver, not taken on the
puller's summary.

## Landed (primary/near-primary)

- **case.21 (Cecilia)** — 1910 US Census, Sheridan Co KS, ED 0187 sheet 6A:
  "Celia A" age 5, daughter of Leonard [indexed "Farrow" = Zodrow] and Katie,
  siblings Leo 10, Mary 8. 1920 census corroborates (Cecilia age 15, same
  household). CLOSED at primary tier; Cecilia's tag -> Documented. Driver read
  the scan directly.
- **case.11 (Dible order)** — John A. Dible's 1866 Westmoreland Co (Penn Twp)
  will, probate case 2663, names eleven children INCLUDING Zephaniah (driver
  read the child-bequest line: "Susannah, Mary Ann, Elizabeth Jane, Ester
  Margaret, Catharine, Zephaniah, John, Malinda, Isabella, William Oliver,
  Albert Franklin"). Confirms John Albert = Zephaniah's father, matching the
  chart. Widow named Rebecah (a later wife; Catherine Heckman earlier).
- **case.06 (Rust)** — RESOLVED to three children: 1900 census mother's column
  3 born/3 living; 1900 + 1905 both show only Francis, Clarence, Willie. The
  fourth linked memorial (Nina Frances) is spurious; daughter enumerated
  "Francis," never "Nina." East Frisian chain upgraded to primary via
  Wybelsum/Wymeer Reformed parish records (Jan Aissen Rust burial 1830; Aisse
  Jansen Rust marriage 1841 naming his father + Aaltje Jakobs).
- **case.01 (Zodrow/Grams)** — 1885 Kansas state census places the household in
  Republic Co (Elk Creek), not Sheridan (so the move came after 1885), with
  Rosa Grams (~80) living in it — corroborates her 1895 death at John Zodrow's
  residence. (Ancestry mis-indexed wife Antonette as a male brother; read the
  image.)

## HELD for the owner — case.07, sensitive (Evelyn's maternity)

Primary records CONFIRM the identity: Talmadge Dewitt Clemans = Michael "Mike"
DeWitt Clemans, b. ~1903 Barron Co WI, son of Rollie Clemans + Ellen (Martin)
(1910 Barron Co census names "Dewitt Talmadge Clemens" age 7; 1930 and 1940
censuses track the same man — incl. a 1930 incarceration at the Minnesota State
Prison, age-at-first-marriage 17 corroborating a ~1921 marriage to Margaret).

BUT the same 1940 census makes his daughter **Marjorie age 18 (b. ~1922)**.
Evelyn Mundell was born January 1935. If that Marjorie is Evelyn's mother, she
was ~13 — and the previously-excluded Indiana Marjorie Clemans (b. 1914)
doesn't fit either. So NEITHER documented "Marjorie Clemans" is a clean mother
for Evelyn. This may indicate a very young motherhood (consistent with Evelyn
being "raised by her grandmother Frances Adolph") OR a same-name mis-attachment
of the whole Clemans grandmother-line. NOT resolvable from these records;
needs Evelyn's own birth record or the 1940 census household Evelyn appears in.
NOTHING about this lands on the page pending the owner's steer; living
descendants mean it is handled with dignity and no living names, ever.
