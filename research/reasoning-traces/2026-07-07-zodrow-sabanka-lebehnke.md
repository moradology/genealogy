# Zodrow Sabanka To Neu Lebehnke Locality Path

## Question

What place does the Leoville cemetery note "Sabanka, Prussia" point to for John M. Zodrow's birth?

## Working Hypothesis

"Sabanka" may be a distorted or locally remembered form tied to the Neu Lebehnke/Lebehnke area in Kreis Deutsch Krone, Westpreussen, now Nowa Lubianka in Poland. The key practical point is not the exact spelling alone, but the parish and Standesamt jurisdiction that can produce original records.

## Why It Is Plausible

- The Leoville Calvary Cemetery transcription gives John M. Zodrow's dates and records the birthplace as Sabanka, Prussia.
- Kartenmeister, Meyers Gazetteer, and AGOFF place Neu Lebehnke and Lebehnke in Kreis Deutsch Krone and identify church/civil jurisdictions.
- The Lebehnke Catholic banns abstract contains collateral Zodrow entries, showing the surname in the relevant parish area.
- A Szukaj w Archiwach catalog lead references a Johann Zodrow property file in Neu Lebehnke dated 1860-1873, potentially useful collateral geography.

## What Is Not Proved

- No original birth/baptism record has yet tied John M. Zodrow to Neu Lebehnke/Lebehnke.
- The Sabanka-to-Neu-Lebehnke identification remains a working locality hypothesis.
- Collateral Zodrow entries do not by themselves prove John M. Zodrow's parents.

## Next Moves

1. Search Lebehnke Catholic and Lutheran parish records for John/Johann Zodrow around 29 Jun 1843.
2. Search Standesamt Lebehnke records after 1874 for collateral Zodrow entries that name older generations.
3. Pull the Neu Lebehnke land/property file for Johann Zodrow if accessible, and test whether names or plot data connect to the family.
4. Search for Grams/Gramms/Antonia/Antonette in nearby West Prussian and Polish locality records.
