# James Mundell Probate Names John as Son

## Question

Was John Mundell a son of the James Mundell whose land trail reaches Clinton
County, Indiana?

## Evidence Read

The Frankfort Community Public Library's index of original Clinton County
loose court papers returns a probate entry dated 14 August 1837 for John
Mundell in James Mundell folder 236A, file 32. Its relationship field says
"son." The same folder's index rows name a wife, co-executor, and children,
including Joseph.

Barry and Woodruff's 1968 genealogy is now available in full. Its transcript
of Clinton County Will Book I, page 22, gives John a small bequest and reports
the same probate date. The official index and compiled transcript therefore
converge on the relationship, although neither substitutes for inspecting the
original record.

## Conclusion

James-to-John is strong at archival-index tier. This does not prove that Harvey
was John's son, and the book's still older Pennsylvania-to-Kentucky connection
remains circumstantial. case.22 stays open at the Harvey-to-John step.

   as John's son.
