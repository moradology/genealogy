# Clemans + Flaherty (Wisconsin) cluster: validation (2026-07-08)

Independent validation of the frontier-intake Clemans/Flaherty cluster
(`research/frontier-intake/11-clemans-flaherty.md`; TODO.md 3.11). All seven
claims re-queried/re-fetched by a validation agent on 2026-07-08 against the
Wisconsin Historical Society pre-1907 vital index (near-primary), a Wayback
capture of the Douglas County GenWeb Calvary transcription (the live wigenweb

## Verdicts

1. **CONFIRMED, strengthened — Rollie C. Clemans m. C. Ellen Martin, 23 Feb 1902,
   Barron Co, Vol 2 Pg 35.** Both spousal records pulled independently (WHS
   MR1985720 and MR1530126) — identical volume/page/date/county, the signature of
   a paired groom/bride entry. Stronger than the intake's "possible spousal match"
   pointer.
2. **CONFIRMED — Michael D. Clemans b. 8 Sep 1902, Barron Co** (WHS BR3346424,
   Reel 0005 Rec 001114).
3. **CONFIRMED — Helen G. Clemans b. 11 Nov 1904; Inez M. Clemans b. 23 Feb 1906,
   Barron Co.** The county's full Clemans set is exactly six records: these two,
   Michael D, Rollie's marriage, Mary (m. 20 Oct 1883), Jacob H (m. 14 Jun 1902).
4. **CONFIRMED with scope caution — Edward J. Flaherty birth 11 Jan 1897** (WHS
   BR2581801, Reel D0006 Rec 14104). The WHS county field literally reads
   "Douglas"; Find a Grave transcribes "Town of Douglas, MARQUETTE County" — do
   NOT assert Douglas County until the microfilm image is pulled.
5. **CONFIRMED verbatim — Martin Flaherty (1867-1934) + Kate Flaherty (1874-1913),
   adjoining Calvary Cemetery stones, plot D6, Superior WI** (Wayback capture
   20240905191915 of the GenWeb transcription). Plot D6 also holds Joseph
   (1898-1935) and Martin Jr. (1904-1962). Caveat: ~30 Flaherty burials on that
   page across many plots — a big same-surname cluster.
6. **CONFIRMED verbatim — the Indiana Marjorie Clemans is a different person.**
   The Eby/Minnich descendants volume (archive.org descendantsofchr00minn):
   Marjorie Clemans b. 1 Mar 1914 Liberty Mills IN, daughter of Charles Clemans +
   Susie Johnson, m. Clay V. Stamper 1935 — a distinct family. case.07 cites this
   as the excluded collision.
7. **CONFIRMED verbatim — the WHS index ends 30 Sep 1907** ("The Society does not
   own vital records after September 30, 1907"), explaining Kate's absent 1913
   death. It does NOT explain Margaret's own claimed 1901 Douglas Co birth being
   absent — that gap is in scope and stands unexplained.
