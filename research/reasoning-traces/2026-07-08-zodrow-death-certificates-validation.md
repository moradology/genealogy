# Zodrow Washington death-certificate cluster: validation (2026-07-08)

Independent validation of the frontier-intake Zodrow-west cluster
(`research/frontier-intake/16-zodrow-west.md`, research/archive/program-board-2026-07-08.md 3.16). The validating agent
fetched the Washington State Digital Archives records itself — including the
actual certificate page SCANS (DigitalObject downloads), not just index HTML —
and reached Find a Grave through a reader proxy after direct and country-mirror
fetches 403'd. Sensitive-material house rule applies throughout: documented fact,
stated with dignity.

## Verdicts

1. **CONFIRMED (from the certificate scan) — Julius Henry Zodrow, State File 6169**
   digitalarchives.wa.gov DigitalObject 8ec9f01a-d9b3-439c-9d17-76b4aa71e578.
   On the form: institution "Eastern State Hospital", Medical Lake; Widowed;
   date of birth "6-9-1870"; occupation "Farmer" / "own farm"; physician attended
   "from 11-15-56 to 3-13-59". New facts from the scan: died 13 Mar 1959, 1:32 PM,
   age 88; length of stay "2yr 3mo 28da"; cause bronchopneumonia (2 days);
   birthplace Wisconsin; father "John Zodrow"; mother's maiden name "unob.";
   residence E. 3008 Rowan Ave, Spokane; funeral director Hazen & Jaeger.
   The birth date 9 Jun 1870 conflicts with the memorial's "June 1869" — land as a
   `vs.` conflict on his row, certificate first.

2. **CONFIRMED with spelling caution — Ernestine Zodrow, DOH death index**
   Record 6DDD34128AF207DE282FD239CB42F43F: died "14 May 1940", Chewelah, Stevens
   County; spouse "H. Zodrow"; father **"August Biaer"** — the record spells it
   Biaer (a/i transposed), and Leonard's certificate repeats that spelling. Land as
   "August Biaer [i.e. Baier]" — first documented parent for Ernestina; opens
   chart slot Z-14/15 with the spelling preserved.

3. **CONFIRMED — Albert L. Zodrow** (record 789B836FE9D97E13A7718CEDF8B3FF98 +
   burial 7AD55FEFC59AB0D14A3EAE2EAE4F51FD): d. 24 Feb 1957, Medical Lake; father
   "Julius H. Zodrow", mother "Ernstina"; buried 27 Feb 1957, Holy Cross Spokane,
   plot B-1101-2/1. A previously unlisted son of Julius and Ernestina.

4. **CONFIRMED (from the scan) — Leonard William Zodrow, 1983**
   DigitalObject 45ee30d4: b. 18 Oct 1897, Kansas (household still in Kansas that
   late); mother "Ernestine Biaer"; buried 13 Jun 1983 Holy Cross, Spokane;
   crusher operator, mining; spouse Frances Hickey; informant Mrs. Gertrude
   Kleinert; conditions include chronic schizophrenia, OBS, CVD (state plainly if
   ever used; house rule).

5. **CONFIRMED with two cautions — August Henry Zodrow, 1981**
   DigitalObject 47f7bf19: b. 16 Jun 1894, Kansas; father "Julius Zodrow"; veteran;
   died V.A. Hospital, Vancouver; remelt fireman, aluminum; buried 19 Feb 1981
   Holy Cross, Spokane; informant "Gordon Zodrow", Woodland WA.
   (a) The certificate does NOT label Gordon a brother — that is an inference from
   the shared father; never cite the certificate for "brother".
   (b) Field 27 gives mother's maiden name as "Augustine", no surname —
   inconsistent with Ernestine/Biaer on the sibling records; almost certainly an
   informant error, but record the discrepancy.

6. **PARTIALLY CONFIRMED — Holy Cross register.** Julius bur. 18 Mar 1959 plot
   B-1101-4/1 and Ernestine bur. "11 May 1940" plot B-1101-3/1 confirmed from
   individual records; five of the claimed ten Zodrow burials corroborated by
   directly-read records. The aggregate "ten burials" list is behind a
   reCAPTCHA-gated search — NOT independently verified; keep at intake tier.
   **Impossible date pair:** cemetery register burial "11 May 1940" vs. DOH death
   index "14 May 1940" — burial cannot precede death; one transcription is wrong.
   Land as: died mid-May 1940, buried Holy Cross plot B-1101-3/1, with the
   11-vs-14 May conflict stated (evidence-honesty rule).

7. **CONFIRMED verbatim — Hedwig "Hattie" Zodrow Schroer, 1961 Lewiston Tribune
   obituary** (Find a Grave memorial 75485451 via reader proxy):
   > "Mrs. Schroer was born Hedwig Zodrow October 15, 1885 at Republic County
   > Kansas the daughter of Mr. & Mrs. John Zodrow." · "She was married to Ben H.
   > Schroer at Leoville Kansas November 13, 1907." · "The couple lived in Kansas
   > until 1911 and then moved to Ferdinand." · survivors "2 brothers, Tony &
   > Leonard Zodrow, Selden Kansas."
   New: exact marriage date 13 Nov 1907; died 1 Jul 1961 of a heart attack at
   home, Ferdinand; Assumption Catholic Church member; sister Mrs. Anna Schroer,
   Dresden, Kansas; three named daughters. The Kansas-to-Idaho move is now dated
   (1911) by a contemporary obituary.
