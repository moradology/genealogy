# Homer Mundell, Marjorie Evelyn Clemans, and the raising of Evelyn (2026-07-08)

Owner-authorized single-session Ancestry pull (their subscription), driven directly by
the main session via headful Chrome/CDP. This trace records the Mundell/Clemans work

## The Homer–Marjorie timeline (now fully documented)

- **19 Jun 1933** — Homer C. Mundell (age given 26) m. Marjorie M. Clemans (age given 21),
  Akron, Washington Co, Colorado. Marriage Record Report no. 1896 (pull 16; CO County
  Marriage Records & State Index, dbid 61366 rec 288450). No parent fields on this form.
- **16 Jan 1935** — daughter Evelyn Delores Mundell born, Flagler, Kit Carson Co, CO.
- **~18 Nov 1936** — son Herbert Duane Mundell born (see below).
- **26 Jun 1939** — Homer C. Mundell divorced **Marjorie Evelyn Mundell**, Colorado.
  Colorado, Divorce Index, 1851-1985 (coll. 60927 rec 1263669). *This is the record that
  gives Marjorie's full middle name: **Evelyn** — the daughter Evelyn's namesake.*
- **by Apr 1940** — Homer remarried **Leora A.** (age 20); household in Colby, Thomas Co KS.
- **6 Feb 1956** — Homer Clair Mundell m. **Clara Isbell Walker**, Colorado Springs, El Paso Co.
  CO Select County Marriages 1863-2018 (coll. 61846 rec 568982).

So Homer married three times: Marjorie (1933–39), Leora (by 1940), Clara (1956). The
"Leora/William Rogers context that needs household-level sorting" flagged on the page is
now RESOLVED: Leora was the second wife (post-Marjorie); Evelyn's siblings named in
Donald Lynn Mundell's obituary (Marilyn Ayers, Judy Bastian, and Donald Lynn himself)
are half-siblings via Leora, not Marjorie's children.

## Homer Clair Mundell — corrected identity

- 1940 census, Colby, Thomas Co KS, sheet 11B HH252 (coll. 2442 rec 58087568): "Homer
  Mandell" [indexed correction: Homer Mundell], age 29, b~1911 KS, Married, Head, MECHANIC,
  lived in Colby in 1935. Wife Leora A. Mandell, 20. No children in the household.
- 1930 census, Cloverly, Big Horn Co WYOMING, sheet 3B (coll. 6224 rec 113054914): Homer C.
  Mundell, age 19, b~1911 KS, Single, LODGER + farm hand in the Myron L. Austin household;
  both parents Kansas-born.
- Ancestry's suggested-records tie all of these to one man via: CO Select County Marriages
  ("Mr Homer Clair Mundell"), WWII Draft Card ("Homer Clair Mundell"), and the CO Divorce
  Index. **CORRECTION: Homer was born ~1911, not ~1907.** The 1933 marriage clerk's "age 26"
  was inflated (+~4y); he was ~22 at marriage. Both parents Kansas-born is consistent with
  Walter William Mundell + Mary Frances Rust.

## THE RAISING OF EVELYN — now PRIMARY (1940 census)

1940 census, Barrett Twp, Thomas Co KS, sheet 2B (coll. 2442 rec 58086643):
- Lon A. Adolph, 58, Head
- **Frances Adolph, 45, Wife** (b~1895, MO) = Mary Frances "Frances" Rust, Homer's mother,
  remarried to Lon Adolph.
- Derane Adolph, 10, Son; Kenneth Adolph, 19
- **Evelyn "Mondel" [Mundell], 5, GRANDDAUGHTER**
- **Herbert "Mondel" [Mundell], 3, GRANDSON**

This is the primary record for Evelyn's obituary statement that she was "raised by
grandmother Frances Adolph." Both of Homer & Marjorie's children (Evelyn and Herbert) were
in the paternal grandmother's household in 1940, after the June 1939 divorce. It also
CONFIRMS the Rust→Adolph identity that was a "working lead": Frances can only have Mundell
grandchildren through her son Homer, so Frances Adolph = Frances Rust (wife of Walter
Mundell, then of Lon Adolph). Note: Frances's birthplace reads "Missouri" here (the page's
Rust cluster is Smith Co KS / Reamsville — a field to reconcile, minor).
