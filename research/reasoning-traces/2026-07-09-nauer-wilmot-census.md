# Nauer and Wand Household in the 1851 Canada Census

## Question

Does the Wilmot Township census prove that Thomas Nauer was a son or stepson
of Lorenz and Dorothea Nauer?

## Evidence Read

The household crosses a page break. Lorenz Nauer, 49, and Thorothea Nauer,
50, are followed by Lorenz and Maria Wand and then Carolina, Jacob, John, and
Maria Nauer. The entries share one hewed-log house and one-family tally. Every
named person is Prussia-born and Roman Catholic by the enumerator's ditto
marks.

Thomas is not in the household. Focused official-index searches found no
Thomas Nauer or plausible surname variant in Wilmot Township, Waterloo County,
or nationwide, though an omission, spelling failure, or separate residence is
still possible.

## Conclusion

The census validates a blended Nauer/Wand household and the literal birthplace
"Prussia." It does not state relationships, prove Thomas's parentage, or settle
whether Dorothea's earlier surname was Meyer or Wand. The parent-child edge in
case.04 remains open.
