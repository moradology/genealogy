# Zimmerman Mainhardt Archion Path

## Question

Can the Zimmerman line be taken from John Paul Zimmerman and Catherine Zinkle/Zinkl back into named parents in Europe?

## Working Hypothesis

The likely European parish target is Mainhardt, Dekanat Schwaebisch Hall, with Wuerttemberger Hof/Huetten as the local place cluster. Michael John Zimmerman Sr. and Paul Michael Zimmerman are the practical bridge because their public profiles point to births there before the family appears in Madison County, Nebraska.

## Why It Is Plausible

- Michael John Zimmerman Sr. is indexed with a 25 Oct 1869 birth at Wuerttemberger Hof/Mainhardt.
- Paul Michael Zimmerman is indexed with the same Mainhardt-area locality pattern.
- The 1880 Madison County, Nebraska household places John, Catherine, Michael, Paul, Baptist, and Louisa together, supporting the parent/child cluster after immigration.
- Archion lists the exact Mainhardt church books needed: baptism, marriage, and family-register volumes.

## What Is Not Proved

- The original baptism entries for Michael and Paul have not yet been read.
- John Paul Zimmerman and Catherine Zinkle/Zinkl's marriage entry has not yet been read.
- John Paul's own 1842 baptism is still only a target unless Band 7 confirms him in that parish.
- The exact spellings and parent names must come from original church-book entries, not public tree repetition.
