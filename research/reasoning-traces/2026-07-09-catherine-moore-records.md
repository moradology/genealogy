# Catherine Moore Dible in Census and Child Records

## Question

Which parts of Catherine Moore Dible's identity and disputed dates can be
supported by original or near-contemporary records?

## Evidence Read

The 1860 census enumerates Catherine Dible, age 21 and Pennsylvania-born, with
Zephaniah Dible in Allegheny Township, Armstrong County. This supports birth
about 1839 and proves she was alive on 12 July 1860.

In 1886, daughter Mary E. Dible signed her own Westmoreland County marriage
application and named her parents Zephaniah and Catherine Dible. A 1901
biographical sketch of son John calls him a son of Zephaniah and Catherine
Moore Dible and says Zephaniah later married Clarinda Stodghill.

## Conclusion

The name, marriage, motherhood, Pennsylvania origin, and approximate 1839
birth are now well supported. The unsourced 1864 death and the competing 1872
claim are not proved. Even the later marriage is dated differently in two
derivative sources, so it supplies only an outer boundary until pulled.
No reliable parents or burial for Catherine surfaced; case.10 remains in
conflict.
