# Cecilia's parents: Leonard Ferdinand Zodrow, not Julius (2026-07-08)

Owner-initiated lead, fetched and verified this session. The page has carried
Cecilia Antoinette Zodrow Zimmerman (Doyle's mother, Z-3) as a daughter of
Julius Henry Zodrow + Ernestina Baier on the strength of public FamilySearch
sibling indexing (compiled-tier, and tagged Strong lead). That is wrong.

## Evidence, as fetched 2026-07-08

1. **Cecilia's memorial (Find a Grave 104955721**, fetched via reader proxy +
   real Chrome): b. 20 Jun 1904, **Leoville, Decatur County, Kansas**; d. 10 Dec
   1997, Colby; buried Sacred Heart Cemetery, Colby. Family grid: parents
   **Leonard Ferdinand Zodrow 1874-1961** and **Catherine Fleckenstein Zodrow
   1874-1961**; spouse Leonard Henry Zimmerman 1903-1968; siblings Leo Julius
   Zodrow 1899-1990, Mary Margaret Zodrow Heisz 1901-1991, Arnold Carl Zodrow
   1916-1985; children include Doyle (34266662).

2. **Leonard Ferdinand Zodrow's memorial (71839302)**, reciprocal: b. 1 Nov
   1874, **Shawano, Shawano County, Wisconsin** (new Wisconsin-sojourn datum —
   Shawano, not Marquette, County); d. 10 Oct 1961, Colby; buried **Selden
   Cemetery**. Parents John Zodrow 1843-1915 + Antonette Grams Zodrow
   1846-1934; spouse Catherine Fleckenstein; siblings include Julius Henry
   Zodrow 1869-1959; children include **Cecilia Antoinette Zodrow Zimmerman
   1904-1997**.

3. **Corroborating negatives already validated at primary tier** (trace
   2026-07-08-zodrow-death-certificates-validation.md): none of the Washington
   death certificates for Julius's household names a Cecilia among his
   children (Albert L., Leonard William, August Henry, Gordon are documented);

Doyle's great-uncle. Tier: two reciprocal memorial family grids
